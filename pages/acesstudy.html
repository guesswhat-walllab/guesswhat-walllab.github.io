Stanford Game App for Autism Research

Help Stanford Test New Autism Therapy


The Wall Lab at Stanford University is enrolling families in a study testing a mobile game designed to improve emotion recognition and social skills.


Eligibility: To participate, you must be an adult parent of a child with ASD between 3 and 12 years old. Participants can live anywhere in the world, but do need a smartphone or tablet with access to WiFi.


Procedure: All study participation is done from home. You will receive a $50 Amazon gift card following completion of all study procedures, and an additional $20 gift card for completion of follow-up measures.
